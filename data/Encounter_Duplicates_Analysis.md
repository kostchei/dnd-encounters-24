## Recommendations

1. **Consider thematic exclusivity**: Some duplicates make sense thematically (e.g., Archmage appearing everywhere), but others dilute regional identity.

2. **Dragons**: Heavy overlap between Calimshan and Heartlands for chromatic/metallic dragons. Consider:
   - Calimshan: Desert dragons (Blue, Brass, Copper)
   - Heartlands: Forest/temperate dragons (Green, Bronze, Gold)
   - IceWind Dale: Cold dragons (White, Silver)

3. **Fiends**: Calimshan and Dungeons share many devils/demons. Consider making Calimshan more genie-focused and Dungeons more aberration-focused.

4. **Lycanthropes**: Split between regions or make one region the "werewolf territory."

5. **Universal monsters** (acceptable duplicates):
   - Tarrasque (world-ending threat)
   - Lich (can appear anywhere)
   - Archmage (universal NPC type)
